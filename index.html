FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do I need a tune to install this intake?

No, a tune is not required to install this intake. The intake is designed to work with the factory ECU calibration. However, for maximum performance gains, we recommend pairing it with an iE tune.

Do I need an intake lid?

The intake lid is an optional upgrade that enhances both the aesthetics and performance of your intake system. It provides additional heat shielding and improved airflow.

Do I have to upgrade the turbo inlet pipe?

While not required for most vehicles, upgrading the turbo inlet pipe is recommended for maximum performance. Some vehicles like the VW Arteon require the iE Turbo Inlet Pipe for intake installation.

When would I need the Harsh Climate Filter?

The Harsh Climate Filter is recommended for drivers in extreme conditions such as dusty environments, heavy rain, or areas with significant airborne debris. It provides additional filtration without sacrificing airflow.

How do I clean the air filter?

Clean the filter using a quality air filter cleaning kit. Apply cleaner, let it soak for 10 minutes, rinse with low-pressure water from the clean side out, let dry completely, then re-oil if using an oiled filter.

Whats in the box?

The intake kit includes: Cold air intake tube, high-flow air filter, heat shield, all necessary mounting hardware, silicone couplers, hose clamps, and detailed installation instructions.
